# Student Guidelines - English Professional Platform

Welcome! This guide will help you navigate the English Professional platform to maximize your learning experience.

## 1. Getting Started
*   **Sign Up/Login**: Create an account or log in at `/accounts/login/`.
*   **Profile**: Complete your profile to personalize your experience.

## 2. Your Dashboard
*   **Overview**: Upon logging in, the **Dashboard** (`/dashboard/`) shows your current courses, progress, and upcoming deadlines.
*   **To-Do List**: Keep an eye on pending quizzes or peer reviews listed here.

## 3. Learning Modules
*   **Courses**: Browse and enroll in courses via the **Courses** tab.
*   **Lessons**: Go through chapters such as "Nouns and Pronouns", "Vocabulary Builder", and "Sentence Building".
*   **Downloads**: Download study materials provided by your instructors.

## 4. Assignments & Quizzes
*   **Taking Quizzes**: Navigate to the **Quiz** section to take tests. Your scores are recorded immediately.
*   **Deadlines**: Be aware of assignment deadlines. The system may send email reminders for upcoming due dates.

## 5. Peer Review
*   **Submissions**: Submit your written assignments in the **Peer Review** section.
*   **Feedback**: You may be required to review assignments from other students. Provide helpful and constructive feedback to earn credit.

## 6. Community & Interaction
*   **Forum**: Use the **Forum** to discuss topics, ask questions about lessons, and interact with peers.
*   **Chat**: Use the real-time **Chat** to message other students or instructors for quick queries.
*   **Announcements**: Check the **Announcements** section regularly for news from your instructors.

## 7. Gamification
*   **Points & Badges**: You earn points for completing lessons, passing quizzes, and participating in forums.
*   **Leaderboard**: Check the leaderboard to see how you rank against other students.

## 8. Technical Tips
*   **Notifications**: Ensure your email address is correct to receive notifications about messages and deadlines.
*   **Support**: If you encounter issues, post in the support forum or contact an instructor via chat.
